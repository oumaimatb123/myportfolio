About Me

Hello! I’m Oumaima Tabbaza, a passionate web developer based in Meknès, Morocco. I specialize in creating beautiful, responsive, and user-friendly websites that provide seamless experiences for users across all devices. With a diploma in Digital Development (Web Full Stack) from ISTA – International School of Technology and Applied Sciences, I have honed my skills in both frontend and backend development. I am proficient in various programming languages, including JavaScript, PHP, and Python, and have experience working with frameworks like React.js and Laravel. My journey in web development began with a deep fascination for technology and design. I love the challenge of transforming ideas into reality, and I thrive on solving problems through code. Continuous learning is at the core of my philosophy; I enjoy keeping up with the latest trends and technologies to enhance my skills and stay ahead in this ever-evolving field. In addition to my technical abilities, I have a keen eye for UI/UX design, which allows me to create visually appealing and intuitive interfaces. My experience in SEO optimization helps ensure that the websites I develop not only look great but also rank well in search engines. When I'm not coding, you can find me reading technical blogs, exploring photography, or working on my design projects. I believe that a good balance between work and personal interests is essential for creativity and inspiration. I’m excited about the opportunity to collaborate on innovative projects and contribute to building engaging digital experiences. Let’s connect and create something amazing together!
